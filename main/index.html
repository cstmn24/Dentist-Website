Our Dentists

Dr. Arun Jayasheel

Expert in Conservative Dentistry, Endodontics & Esthetics.

Dr. Arun Jayasheel is a highly skilled Endodontist and Cosmetic Dentist, currently a Professor in the Department of Conservative Dentistry & Endodontics at Bapuji Dental College & Hospital, Davangere.

He earned his MDS (2012) from Bapuji Dental College and BDS (2009) from AME’s Dental College, Raichur

As a Key Opinion Leader (KOL) and certified trainer, he collaborates with leading global brands like Dentsply Sirona (Endodontics & Aesthetics), Novalase (Laser Dentistry), and Ergoptics (Magnification). His expertise in advanced dental technologies helps train professionals worldwide.

Dr. Arun is also an Executive Committee Member of the Academy of Cosmetic Dentistry India, affiliated with the American Academy of Cosmetic Dentistry (AACD). Passionate about esthetic excellence and innovation, he ensures every patient gets the best in modern dentistry with a personalized approach.

Dr. Thushara Mohanan

PERIODONTIST AND IMPLANTOLOGIST

Dr. Thushara Mohanan is a skilled Periodontist and Implantologist, currently an Assistant Professor in the Department of Periodontology at Bapuji Dental College & Hospital.

She holds an MDS (2017) from CODS, RGUHS University, and a BDS (2011) from Bapuji Dental College. A Rajiv Gandhi University rank holder, she excelled in multiple subjects, including Orthodontics and Surgery.

Dr.Thushara specializes in laser-assisted gum care and advanced dental implantology, ensuring precision, comfort, and long-lasting results for her patients.
